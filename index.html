📋 Terms & Research Consent

Please read before creating your account.

About This App & Study

PAUSE App is a digital health screening and research tool developed at the Department of Community Medicine, Government Medical College, Maheshwaram, Telangana, India. By creating an account you are voluntarily participating in an academic research study on digital health behaviours.

Eligibility

You must be 18 years or older and able to read English. This app is not suitable for individuals currently in mental health crisis.

What Data We Collect
  • Your email address — for account login and data sync only
  • Your profile details — age, gender, occupation, etc. (research variables)
  • Your assessment scores — disorder screening results and Digital Wellness Score
  • Your app usage — logbook, challenge, mood check data (stored locally)

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, camera, or microphone.

How Your Data Is Used
  • Your anonymised data will be used for academic research on digital disorder prevalence
  • Aggregated findings may be published in journals or a PhD thesis
  • Your individual data is never published in identifiable form
  • Your data is never sold or shared with advertisers
Your Rights
  • Withdraw any time — no penalty or consequence
  • Access & delete your data on request
  • Edit your profile at any time within the app
⚠️ Clinical Disclaimer

PAUSE App is a screening tool for educational purposes only. It does not constitute a clinical diagnosis or treatment. If you score Moderate or Severe on any scale, please consult a qualified mental health professional.
