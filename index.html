💡 Why We Built This

PAUSE — Preventive Approach to Unhealthy Screen Exposure — was born out of a simple observation. In community medicine, we treat populations, not just patients. And what we kept seeing was invisible — people anxious from midnight health searches, students fragmenting their attention across endless feeds, professionals unable to switch off. The harm was real. The measurement tools weren't. So we built one.

🏥
Not a tech company. A community medicine department that got tired of watching a problem go unmeasured — and decided to do something about it.

📐 Built on Evidence, Not Opinion

PAUSE doesn't estimate your digital health — it measures it. Every scale used in this app has been peer-reviewed, validated on real populations, and proven to reliably detect genuine disorder. Your Digital Wellness Score (DWS) is a composite of all six, giving you a single number that reflects your overall digital wellbeing.

🗺️ You're Part of Something Larger

India has no large-scale data on digital health disorders. None. Every assessment you complete adds a real data point to what will become the first population-level picture of this — one that could shape school curricula, workplace policies, and public health guidelines for years to come.

Your results are fully anonymised, never sold, and used for exactly one purpose — making these tools better for everyone who comes after you. That's a promise made by doctors, not a policy written by lawyers.
💙 Before You Act on Your Results
PAUSE helps you see clearly. Your doctor helps you act. If any score lands in the Moderate or Severe range, treat that as a nudge — not a diagnosis — and speak to a healthcare professional who can give you the right support.

What the score means
80 – 100  Excellent — very healthy digital habits
65 – 79  Good — mostly healthy with minor concerns
50 – 64  Moderate Risk — some habits need attention
